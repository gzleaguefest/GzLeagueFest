WELCOME TO GAMEZONE MAHDIA
We are honored by your presence at our national League Fest Event

ABOUT THE EVENT

League Fest is the kick starter edition of the e-sports competitions series organized by Game Zone iset and isi mahdia.

This action takes place in 2 phases, an online phase and an offline phase.

The online phase will be broadcasted on streaming websites, then replays will be available on our social media accounts. The offline phase will consist of a tournament and a Festive Celebration at the evening of the finals.


And to top it off, there will be coverage of the day by 'MFM' Mahdia radio.
