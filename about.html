CLUBS PRESENTATION

Game Zone is a university gaming & Game-Dev club created in 2020 at ISET Mahdia.
The club consists of 7 branches around Tunisia:
GameZone isetma, isima, isims, isetna, tekup, isgis, lmb, istic.
The club offers services for players including latest video games and a passionate community of players, it also offers young developers training in this field.
